Overview

Can you believe that you can forseen the future with the help of statsitcis and machine learning models?

With this website you can not only forseen the suburbs with the greatest rental price growth rate but also know the the population, GDP rate, income rate, saving rate, crime rate for suburbs for the next 5 years in Victoria, Australia.

If you are currently looking for the most livable or affordable house or apartment to rent, this website is perfect for you!

You can enter the rank of your prioritization in terms of education, transportation, safety, shopping followed by your budget and our system will automatically return the most liveable and affordable suburb for you. If you are just interested in data science and machine learning models just like us, feel free to explore:)

About us

We are Data Science students are The University of Melbourne. This website is built up in terms of statistical model illustrations. We hope this website can bridge the communcation between the general population and our model and deliver some insights on rental price and liveability. Particularly, we hope that non-data-science background people can enjoy using and exploring this website. So that models constructed by us, the future data scientsts are being applied and realized on a larger scale.
